About the anthology

Diary of a Heartbreak Kid (DAHK) is a poetry anthology fifteen years in the making. Spanning from 2011 to 2026, the collection chronicles the evolution of the human heart through seven distinct emotional climates:

  • Bloom
  • Wilt
  • Dry
  • Thorns
  • Ashes
  • Roots
  • Epiphany

About the experience

“DAHK: The Experience” seeks to transcend the traditional book launch. By merging cinematic media, performance art, and sensory exhibition, we aim to transform Salamatu Adamu’s written words into a visceral, living journey of love, loss, and self-rediscovery.
